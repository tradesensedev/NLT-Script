### 1. First 15 Seconds = Everything
Opening e retention 60%+ rakhate **shokto hook** lage. Amar improved opening e "koyta raat ghum hoyeni" question ta emotional pain hit kore.

### 2. Pattern Interrupts Every 30-45 Seconds
- Visual change (zoom in, screen movement)
- Sound effect (swoosh, ding)
- Text overlay popup
- Camera angle change
- B-roll cut

### 3. Emotional Triggers use korun:
- **Curiosity gap:** "Ja keu bole ni"
- **Fear:** "5 bochor sikheo profit hobe na"
- **Identity:** "Bangladesh e koyjon billionaire?"
- **Controversy:** "ICT = Scam?"
- **Conspiracy:** "Bollei tader business bondho"

### 4. B-Roll / Visual Moments
Ei moment gulo e B-roll obossoi dekhan:
- Loss screenshot (red candles zoom)
- Gold chart (Section 7 er gold example)
- Chessboard (Section 7 metaphor)
- ICT setup rules list animation
- Trading plan template preview

### 5. Retention Hooks Throughout
- "Tritio reason ta sunle chair theke pore jaben" (curiosity)
- "Ekta backtest result e apni shocked hoye jaben"
- "Sesh e ekta bonus tips debo" (open loop)

### 6. Thumbnail + Title Combo
Thumbnail e **face expression** + **red text** + **chart** — ei 3 combo highest CTR dey.

### 7. Chapter Markers Add Korun
YouTube description e chapter timestamps den:
```
00:00 - ICT ki tahole Scam?
01:30 - March 2026 Backtest Proof
03:00 - Dark Side #1: Kingless Chess
05:30 - Dark Side #2: Pattern Trap
08:00 - Dark Side #3: No Plan
10:30 - Asol Solution
```
Eta watch time barai.

### 8. Comment Prompt
Video er moddhe ekbar bolun:
> "Apni ICT sikhte sikhte koyta raat ghum hoyeni — comment e bolun. Ami prottekta reply debo."

Comment engagement YouTube algorithm e huge push dey.

### 9. Community Tab
Video release er 24 hour age Community Post kore teaser den:
> "Kalke ekta video ashche jeta ICT sikher apnar dharona pure change kore dibe. Prepared thakun…"

### 10. Length Recommendation
Ei topic er jonno **10-14 minute** optimal. Besi long hole retention drop korbe, kom hole depth thakbe na.
